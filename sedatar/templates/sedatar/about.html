About this website

The purpose of this website is to provide simple schematic depictions of known planetary systems, alongside with some additional information about the planets and their host stars. Planetary and stellar data is provided by NASA's exoplanet archive and is taken 'as is'. This is a hobbyist project and makes no claims about being complete, correct, consistent or even useful.

A list of third-party libraries used by this project can be found here.
